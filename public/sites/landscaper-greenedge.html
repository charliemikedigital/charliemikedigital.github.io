A season of growth

Your yard, year-round.

Houston's gentle climate means twelve months of growing — and twelve months of keeping up. Here's how we care for your property through each season.

March — May

Spring

Wake your yard up from winter. Fresh mulch, first mow, and beds set for the season.

  • Spring cleanup
  • Pre-emergent weed control
  • Mulch refresh & edging
  • Annual plantings
June — August

Summer

Protect your investment through Houston heat. Smart irrigation, careful mowing, no scorched lawns.

  • Weekly mowing & edging
  • Irrigation check-ups
  • Drought-tolerant upgrades
  • Shrub shaping
September — November

Autumn

Crisp edges, clean beds, and a jump on leaf season before it gets away from you.

  • Fall cleanup & leaf haul
  • Overseeding & aeration
  • Bulb planting
  • Irrigation winterizing
December — February

Winter

Houston winters are mild, but plants still need tending. Dormant pruning and planning for spring.

  • Dormant tree & shrub pruning
  • Storm debris cleanup
  • Landscape design consults
  • Hardscape projects
