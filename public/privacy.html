Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Charlie Mike Digital LLC ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit charliemikedigital.com or contact us, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

Information we collect

Information you give us directly

When you fill out the contact form, you provide your name, email address, optional phone number, the project type you're interested in, and a short message describing your business. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and to provide the services you request.

Information collected automatically

Like any website, ours receives standard log information (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL) through our hosting provider, Cloudflare. This is used for security, fraud prevention, and basic traffic analytics. We don't use it for advertising or marketing tracking.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries you submit through the contact form.
  • To send you proposals, quotes, or follow-up communication about a project you've asked about.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We use the following services that may receive limited data on our behalf:

  • Formspree processes contact form submissions. See their policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Cloudflare provides hosting, CDN delivery, and basic analytics. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
  • Google Fonts loads typography from Google's CDN. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies. We don't use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set technical cookies for security (bot detection) and performance.

Your rights

You can request a copy of any personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, or object to our use of it, by emailing matt@charliemikedigital.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Children

This site isn't directed to children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy
