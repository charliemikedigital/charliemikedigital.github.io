Our Story

Rooted in Houston.
Inspired by season.

Mesa opened in 2018 with one simple idea — use the best local ingredients we can find, treat every guest like family, and never stop trying to get better.

Our menu changes with the seasons. Our relationships with our farmers and ranchers don't. Every dish tells you where it came from because we think that matters — the Gulf shrimp, the grass-fed ribeye, the heirloom tomatoes in August.

Come hungry. Stay a while. The best meals are the ones you don't want to end.

Daniel Reyes
— Chef Daniel Reyes Executive Chef & Owner · Since 2018
