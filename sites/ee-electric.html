What We Do

Electrical Services for Every Job, Big or Small

From routine outlet upgrades to complete panel replacements and smart home installs, our team handles every detail with precision and safety in mind.

Panel Upgrades and Replacement

Modernize your electrical panel for safer power, more capacity, and full compliance with current code requirements.

Recessed and Accent Lighting

Custom lighting design and installation, from elegant recessed cans to layered accent lighting that transforms a room.

Security Camera Wiring

Professional installation of flood light cameras, surveillance systems, and the wiring runs to support them cleanly.

Ceiling Fan Installation

Mount, balance, and wire ceiling fans with the right support and switching, indoor or outdoor patio applications.

Smart Home and Lighting Controls

Smart switches, dimmers, and integrated lighting controls installed and configured so everything just works.

Troubleshooting and Repair

Tripped breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights, or hot wiring. We diagnose the root cause and fix it the right way.

Whole Home Rewiring

Replace aging or unsafe wiring with modern, code compliant systems. Cleanly routed, properly grounded, fully tested.

Outdoor and Gate Power

Run dedicated power for gates, landscape lighting, outdoor outlets, and detached structures, weatherproofed and code compliant.

24/7 Emergency Electrical

Power out, sparks, or burning smell? Call any hour, day or night, and we will respond fast to keep you safe.
