FAQ

Questions I Get All the Time

Do I actually own the site when it's done?
Yes. 100%. The code, the design, the domain, the hosting account. All of it is yours. I hand you the keys and you can do whatever you want with it. No lock in, no proprietary platform, no monthly ransom to keep your own site. If you want to walk away tomorrow you take everything with you.
How do you build a website in 72 hours?
I've built enough sites for enough industries that I know exactly what works. I'm not starting from zero every time. I have a proven process and I stick to it. The 30 minute discovery call gives me everything I need, and I get to work immediately after. No committees, no approval chains, no waiting around.
What if I already have a domain?
We use it. I'll point your existing domain to the new site. If you don't have one, I'll help you pick one out and get it set up. Either way it's included.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
Every package includes two rounds of revisions. You see the full site before anything goes live. I don't launch until you're completely happy with it. Most clients love the first version, but if something needs to change, we change it.
What happens if I need changes later?
If you're on a care plan, edits are included depending on your tier. If you're not on a plan, I do one off edits at an hourly rate. Or since you own the site, you can edit it yourself or hire anyone else. You're never stuck with me.
Do you do SEO?
Every site includes basic SEO setup, which covers page titles, meta descriptions, mobile optimization, fast load times, and proper heading structure. That alone is more than most small business sites have. Full ongoing SEO campaigns are a different service, but the foundation is built in from day one.
